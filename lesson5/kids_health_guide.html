💪 why your body needs exercise

Your body is like a super car — the more you use it the stronger it gets! kids who move for 60 minutes a day are happier, sleep better , and do better at school.

Dancing, Swiming, Football, Yoga — anything that gets your heart moving and have fun!

☀ quick health tips

  • 🍎 Eat 5 fruit and veggies every day
  • 💧 drink 8 glases of water daily
  • 😴 sleep 9-11 hours nightly
  • 🏃 move body every 60 minutes
